Subpart 623.2 - Energy and Water Efficiency and Renewable Energy

Source: 80 FR 6921, Feb. 9, 2015, unless otherwise noted.

Parent topic: PART 623 - ENVIRONMENT, ENERGY AND WATER EFFICIENCY, RENEWABLE ENERGY TECHNOLOGIES, OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY, AND DRUG-FREE WORKPLACE

623.204 Procurement exemptions.

The head of the contracting activity is the agency head's designee for the purpose of executing the written determination to not purchase ENERGY STAR® or FEMP-designated products.”
